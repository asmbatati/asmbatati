### 👋 About me

Senior Research Assistant at the **Robotics &amp; Internet of Things Lab, Prince Sultan University** (Riyadh), with an **MSc in Robotics from KFUPM** — heading next into a **PhD at KFUPM**. I build flying and driving robots, and the perception that keeps them localized when GPS drops out.

- 🚁 **Now:** drone-to-drone tracking, GPS-denied state estimation, agentic multi-UAV coordination
- 🧠 **Interests:** state estimation (EKF · VIO), trajectory prediction, ROS 2, sim-to-real, Arabic NLP
- 🛠️ **Built:** 8+ robots &amp; UAVs · 2 granted patents · ~10 peer-reviewed papers
- 🌱 Everything here is real research code — learning and building in public.
