About

From the CAD seed to the first flight.

Six years turning mechanical design, UAV autonomy, and reinforcement learning into machines that work in the real world — drones, ground robots, and the perception kits that keep them located when GPS goes dark.

It started as a childhood dream of being an inventor — not an engineer. The machines just made it real.

M.Sc. Robotics (KFUPM) · Senior Research Assistant at Prince Sultan University · heading to a PhD at KFUPM. Based in Riyadh, moving to Khobar.
